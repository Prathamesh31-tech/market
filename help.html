Direct Market Access App for Farmers

Welcome to the Direct Market Access App for Farmers! This guide will walk you through the features and functionality of the app to ensure a smooth experience.

Getting Started


1. Registration

✅To use the app, you must first register an account:
✅Open the app and click on Register.
✅ Provide the required details such as Name, Email, Phone Number, and Password.
✅Select whether you are a Buyer or Farmer.
✅ Click Submit to create your account.

2. Login

✅Once registered, you can log in:
✅Enter your Email and Password.
✅Click Login to access your account.


User Selection

After logging in, you will see two options: ✅Buyer: If you are a buyer, you will be directed to fill in the Buyer Information Form.
✅Farmer: If you are a farmer, you will be taken directly to the dashboard.

Dashboard Features

For Farmers, the dashboard provides various essential tools:
1.Live Weather Updates:- Get real-time weather updates for better farming decisions.
2.Crop Recommendation System:- Receive suggestions for the best crops to grow based on soil and weather conditions.
3.Live Market Prices:- Stay updated on the latest market prices for various agricultural products.
4.Connect with Buyers:- The main feature of the app that enables direct farmer-to-buyer connections.

Connecting Farmers and Buyers

✅The dashboard displays different product categories such as Vegetables, Fruits, Flowers, etc. ✅Click on any category to see available buyers.
✅Connect with buyers to negotiate and finalize deals.

We hope this guide helps you navigate the app efficiently. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to our support team. Happy trading!
